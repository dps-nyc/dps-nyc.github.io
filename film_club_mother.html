The Premiere Experience
 
The opportunity to attend movie premieres is a unique benefit of Film Club membership not readily available to the general public.

Here are several things you should know in advance about the premiere experience:
•  Premieres are logistically complex events involving multiple audiences. Theater staff or studio representatives will direct you to a specific check-in line. We will inform you in advance of any special instructions, and appreciate your willingness to be flexible and patient once you arrive on-site.
•  Our limited ticket allotment will likely be assigned seating in the front, back or side of the venue. (Prime seats are often reserved for movie talent, their guests and other V.I.P.s.)
•  Premieres typically start later than the stated screening time due to the vagaries of star arrivals, red carpet photos and other press obligations.
